A simple guide on how to make D&D dice

I've been making resin D&D dice for about 4 years now, going on 5 next summer. Over that time I've learned some tips and tricks on making the process easier.

One of the big things to note while working with resin is to make sure you're taking proper precautions with PPE- Personal Protection Equipment. Resin is toxic, and prolonged exposure can lead to health problems.


Materials

  • Below is a list of BASIC materials to make resin dice:
    1. Dice Mold
    2. Craft Resin
    3. Alcohol Ink
    4. Acrylic Paint (To paint in the numbers)
    5. Something to protect your work environment
  • The following are the MINIMUM items of PPE that you'll need:
    1. Respirator (Specifically a Natural Vapors respirator)
    2. Gloves
    3. Well-Ventilated Work Environment
  • The last items are tools to take your dice to the next level:
    1. Pressure Pot (To remove bubbles in the resin while curing)
    2. Compressor (For the pressure pot)
    3. Glitter
    4. Polishing Papers (I use Zona polishing papers, after the dice are cured this makes them shine like glass)
    5. Mini Pottery Wheel (To make polishing easier)
    6. More Alochol Inks (To color your dice more colors)
    7. Heat Gun (To avoid bubbles in the dice)
    8. Restraint (Because this rabbit hole goes down forever)
